Help & Guides

    Step-by-step instructions for using our reservation system

    Guide 1: How to Book a Reservation

    1. Click the Amenities in the navigation bar.
    2. Select your desired service type (Room or Venue).
    3. Choose your preferred Duration/Time-Slot for the selected service.
    4. Add one or more amenities to your Selected Items section if you wish to reserve multiple services at once.
    5. Review your selected items. You can remove individual items or clear all before proceeding.
    6. Click Proceed to Reservation Form to fill out your personal information and submit your reservation.
    7. After submitting the reservation form, complete the payment process by providing your GCash reference number and uploading your payment receipt.
    8. Once payment is submitted, your selected items will be automatically cleared, and you will receive a notification that your payment is pending admin verification.

    Guide 2: How to Reschedule a Reservation

    Important Policy: You must submit your reschedule request at least 14 days before your reserved check-in date. This policy ensures the resort can efficiently manage bookings and provide fair opportunities for all guests. If your check-in date is today, you can only reschedule to a date that is at least 14 days from now.

    1. Go to the My Reservations page using the navigation bar.
    2. Find the reservation you wish to reschedule and click the Reschedule button (if available for that reservation).
    3. Select your new desired date, time, or amenity as prompted by the system.
      Note: The system will only allow you to pick a new date that is at least 14 days from today, in accordance with the resort's rescheduling policy.
    4. Submit your reschedule request. The system may require admin approval before the changes take effect.
    5. You will receive an email notification once your reschedule request has been approved or if further action is needed.

    Guide 3: How to View Reservations

    1. Log in to your account.
    2. Click the My Reservations link in the navigation bar.
    3. On the My Reservations page, you can view all your current and past reservations, including their status (e.g., Pending, Paid, Confirmed, Checked In, Completed, Cancelled).
    4. You will also receive email notifications for any updates to your reservation status.

    Guide 4: How to Check-in with/using QR Code

    1. After your reservation is confirmed by the admin, you will receive an email with a unique QR Code for your booking.
    2. Save the email or take a screenshot of the QR Code.
    3. Upon arrival at the resort, present your QR Code to the staff.
    4. The staff will scan your QR Code using the QReserve system to instantly verify your booking and process your check-in.
