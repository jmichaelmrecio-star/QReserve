Help & Guides

Step-by-step instructions for using our reservation system

Guide 1: How to Book a Reservation

  1. Click the Amenities in the navigation bar.
  2. Select your desired service type (Room or Venue).
  3. After selecting the service type you want, choose your desired Duration/Time-Slot.
  4. Once you have decided the amenity and duration of your liking, click Proceed to Reservation Form button.
  5. Fill out the reservation form with your personal information, then submit the form to complete your booking.
  6. After submitting the form, complete the payment process to finalize your reservation.

Guide 2: How to Check-in with/using QR Code

  1. After confirming your reservation, you will be shown a unique QR Code.
  2. Save this page or take a screenshot of the QR Code.
  3. Upon arrival at the resort, present the QR Code to the staff member.
  4. The staff will scan the QR Code using the QReserve system to instantly verify your booking details and process your check-in seamlessly.

Guide 3: How to View Reservations

Registered users can view their current and past reservations.

  1. Ensure you are logged into your account.
  2. Click the Profile icon in the navigation bar.
  3. Click the My Profile
  4. Scroll down until you see My Recent Reservations section to view your reservation details and history.

Guide 4: How to Reserve Multiple Services at Once

You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled check-in time.

  1. Click the Cart button in the navigation bar.
  2. Review the items in your cart to ensure they are correct.
  3. To remove an item, click the Remove button next to the item you wish to delete.
  4. To clear your cart, click the Clear Cart button.
  5. Once you have reviewed and are satisfied with your cart, click the Proceed to Checkout button to finalize your reservation.
